# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Casual-to-dedicated word game players who enjoy risk-reward mechanics. They play daily for streaks, compete on weekly leaderboards, and seek the thrill of high-stakes guessing. The casino metaphor turns each game into a transaction — every guess costs chips, every win pays out.

## Product Purpose

CUSP turns Wordle into a casino experience. Players wager chips, climb comps tiers, build streaks, and compete on weekly leaderboards. The core loop: guess words → win chips → unlock harder rooms → earn status. Success means daily active retention, competitive engagement, and a social layer through The Board and shareable Player Cards.

## Brand Personality

Sleek. Tense. Reward-driven.

Voice is confident and seductive — casino copy without the sleaze. "On the edge of a win" captures the moment-between-moments tension. The brand winks at you; it doesn't bark. Dark glamour, neon confidence, zero apology.

## Anti-references

No specific anti-references. Avoid cartoonish casino imagery (dice, roulette wheels, playing card suits as decoration). No generic "game app" pastels or playful rounded everything. Stay in the premium dark register: tension isn't cute.

## Design Principles

1. **Every guess is a transaction.** The chip economy is the emotional engine. Wins feel like payouts, not points. Losses feel like table stakes, not failure. Every UI element should reinforce the stakes without overwhelming them.

2. **Show the edge.** The interface lives in the gap between risk and reward. Streak multipliers, comps progression, row payouts — make the math visible. Players should always know what they're playing for and what they stand to lose.

3. **Dark glamour, not Vegas kitsch.** The aesthetic is a private high-stakes room, not a casino floor. Deep greens, neon accents, precise typography. Restraint over ornament. The luxury is in the space between elements, not on top of them.

4. **Status is the second currency.** Tiers, trophies, streak badges, leaderboard rank — these carry as much weight as chips. Make progression tangible. Every player should know where they stand and what's next.

5. **Rhythm over noise.** Login streaks, daily comps, weekly leaderboard resets — the product runs on cadence. Don't overwhelm with notifications. Let the natural rhythm of resets and rewards pull players back.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA target. Dark theme with sufficient contrast ratios on all text and interactive elements. Reduced motion support for animations (framer-motion respects `prefers-reduced-motion`). Color is not the sole indicator of state (tile states use pattern + color).
